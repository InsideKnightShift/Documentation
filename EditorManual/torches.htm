TORCHES

 

Torches are used as a light source. They have their usage not only in dark, underground tunnels but also on on-ground territory. On-ground torches are lit automatically right after dusk.

 

When placing torches you need to remember to put them correctly according to the quarters of the world..  The buttons: N, E, W, S are used for this purpose.

Attention You should not place a torch in the middle of a field because it will limit transit.: In order to place a torch in the right way you are required to use the afore-mentioned buttons or the Enter key from the keypad
