WATER

To flood lower parts of map with water, you need to go firstly to the Terrain Tab and click on the Water button, then place the cursor on the map. Finally press the left mouse button to start increasing the water level. Holding the left mouse button pressed causes continuous water rising. To reduce water level you must simultaneously hold the left mouse button and the SHIFT key pressed.

In  case you should happen to flood a greater area than you intended to, you can use the Undo option. You can also remove all the water by pressing  the  Delete Water button.

Notice 1: The water object we use in the game is very realistic, this means that if we flood a large, strongly folded  territory and next reduce the water lever, the water remains in depression and fissures forming lakes.

Notice 2: It is advisable to keep the same water level on one map.
